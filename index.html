The Man, The Myth.

Hi! My name is Eugene Lim, and I'm an aspiring game developer currently studying at Nanyang Polytechnic's Game Development and Technology Course, in hopes of either working for a studio one day, or learning enough to get started on my own big projects.

I really love playing video games and learning how they work. Some of my recent favourites outside of the FPS genre (which will always hold a place in my heart,) include Turn-Based and Fighting Video Games.

You can scroll down to see what I'm working on, or go to the other pages to learn more about me.

The Legend, a Project.

I'm looking to learn Unity more deeply so that I can work on a game I'm calling Criminal Courier, where you play as a courier who delivers packages to criminals while navigating a bustling city, and spending your free-time to either upgrade your business or for recreational activities like talking to friends!

Hopefully I can share more details soon, when I start development, create a gameplay prototype, and release it on Itch.io!

The Other.

I have a creative writing project I hope can be turned into a Table-Top RPG at some point, and it is currently the setting for an existing D&D Game me and some friends are playing in.

The premise is that the world was hit by a destructive comet that changed the planet's landscape, gifted people with newfound powers, created a new resource in the form of crystals varying in rarity and power, alongside introducing the world to uniquely horrifying monsters and the difficulty of the new world.

I'm hoping to either publicly release it as a campaign setting for others to use in the future, or use it as a setting for a story-focused game project in the future.
